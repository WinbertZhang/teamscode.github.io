Our mission
Teamscode was founded by Chris Elliott and Alan Bi of Mercer Island High School. Together, Chris and Alan share a passion for computer programming and a desire to spread the importance of understanding programming in achieving future success. Teamscode works to to expand and express their love of the field of computer science by involving the Mercer Island community as well as the neighboring regions through tutoring and programming contests.
