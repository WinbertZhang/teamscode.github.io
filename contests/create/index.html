Create your own contest!
We empower students by helping them create their own programming contests! We give high schoolers the resources and knowledge needed to join our team and create official TeamsCode events. We can work with your schedule to help you make an awesome programming contest, bootcamp, or hackathon! Creating a contest will require several months and a large amount of work, so start early and be prepared to spend a lot of time. In addition, we require a solid commitment to hosting a contest. In order to guide you through the process, we must ensure that you are willing to work and support the organization.
